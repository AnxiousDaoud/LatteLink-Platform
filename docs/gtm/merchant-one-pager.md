# LatteLink: Mobile Ordering For Independent Coffee Shops

A simple, white‑label mobile ordering platform built only for coffee shops. Launch fast, keep your brand, and keep your payment provider. Flat, predictable pilot pricing.

## The Problem
Independent coffee shops lose orders to long lines and clunky third‑party apps. Generic marketplaces take a cut, weaken margins, and dilute your brand.

## Our Approach
- Coffee‑only focus: product decisions are tailored to café workflows.
- White‑label: your logo, your menu, your pickup flow.
- Keep your processor: continue using your existing Clover setup for payments during pilot.
- Operate simply: owner/staff dashboard for menu, store settings, and live order management.

## What The Pilot Includes
- Customer app: browse menu, customize drinks, order ahead, and track status.
- Staff tools: receive orders, progress through prep → ready → completed.
- Owner dashboard (single store): edit menu items/prices/visibility; basic store settings.
- Notifications: order status updates for customers.
- Deployment: hosted pilot stack with a single coffee location.

## Onboarding Steps (1–2 Days)
1. Kickoff call (30 min): confirm menu source, pickup instructions, and branding assets.
2. Merchant setup: connect your existing Clover merchant account; we do not change your rates.
3. Menu load: import or sync your menu; quick QA pass for options/modifiers.
4. Staff dry run: place a test order; walk through ticket flow at the bar.
5. Go live: share your ordering link/QR with regulars.

## Flat‑Fee Economics (Pilot)
- No % of sales, no surprise add‑ons.
- Simple pilot fee structure; processing fees stay with your existing provider.
- Pricing finalized with you in kickoff; you can cancel anytime.

> Note on payments: Today we support Clover for pilot processing. Additional processors can be evaluated post‑pilot. We do not claim Stripe or marketplace integrations in this phase.

## Why Coffee Shops Choose LatteLink
- Faster lines, happier regulars, higher ticket consistency.
- Keep your brand front and center.
- Change prices and item visibility without an app store release.

## What We Need From You
- Brand assets (logo, brand color), pickup instructions.
- Menu spreadsheet or access to your current menu export.
- Merchant contact for Clover confirmation.
